About Nicholas Stephens


I got serious about photography when I was 16. I love being outside and I love the never ending challenge of trying to portray ‘that feeling’, you know, the warm evenings at twilight, the sense of isolation when you have a mountain to yourself, the sound of waves lapping the shore of an empty beach, seeing the different animals and there different Personalitys. My images are all about the outdoors and telling a story.
